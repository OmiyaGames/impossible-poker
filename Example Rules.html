Examples of a starting rule-set:

  1. Classic ruleset:
    1. highest cumulative card values wins the pot.
    2. clubs gives 10 extra points on top of the card's value.
  2. Difficult ruleset:
    1. Lowest cumulative card value (described below) wins the pot (It can be negative)
    2. Card values are subtracted from each other (First - Second)
  3. MAGFest ruleset:
    1. Highest single card wins
    2. Red cards add one extra value
  4. Difficult ruleset:
    1. Cumulative sum of card values are as close to 13 as possible
    2. Aces are valued as 1.
  5. Weird ruleset:
    1. Pairs looses instantly
    2. The first player that screams, "Auugh!" wins, regardless of the played cards. The rulemaster must say "Auuuugh!" at one point in the game.
