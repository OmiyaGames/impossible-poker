Pre-requisites:
1) 4 or more people.
2) There's one rulemaster.
3) Other players receive 5 chips.
4) Rulemaster starts with 3 rules.

Rules are:
1) All players start with 3 cards each round.
2) All players chip in at least one chip in the beginning.
3) If one player plays the most chips than others, the other players must call or raise.
4) All player plays one card.
5) Rulemaster, going by the rules, distributes the money to the winner (there is always one winner).
